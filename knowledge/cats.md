# Cats in Minecraft Bedrock

## Finding and taming a cat

Stray cats appear around populated villages, and black cats can appear at swamp huts. To tame a cat, hold raw cod or raw salmon and approach slowly so it does not run away. Use the raw fish on the cat until hearts and a collar appear; taming can take more than one fish.

## What a tamed cat does

A tamed cat follows its owner unless it is told to sit. Cats frighten creepers and phantoms. A cat that sleeps near its owner may leave a small gift when the owner wakes up.

This card summarizes Mojang's official [Cat article](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/article/cat). The current Bedrock vanilla entity data independently lists fish and salmon as cat taming items.
